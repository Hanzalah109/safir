Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and for some regions, we also accept payment through local methods. All payments are securely processed through encrypted channels to ensure your financial information is protected.

Can I change plans after signing up?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new rate will apply immediately. If you downgrade, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. There are no fees for changing plans.

Do you offer family discounts?

Absolutely! We offer a 15% discount for each additional family member enrolled in our programs. This applies to siblings, spouses, and children. Contact our support team to set up a family plan and maximize your savings while providing Quranic education for your entire family.

Are there any long-term contracts?

No, we don't believe in locking students into long-term contracts. Our plans are month-to-month, giving you the flexibility to pause or cancel at any time. However, we do offer additional discounts for students who commit to 3, 6, or 12-month plans to help them save while maintaining consistency in their learning journey.

What if I need to take a break from classes?

We understand that life can be unpredictable. You can pause your subscription for up to 3 months without losing your progress or place with your teacher. Simply notify us at least 7 days before your next billing cycle, and we'll put your account on hold. When you're ready to resume, your teacher and learning materials will be waiting for you.
