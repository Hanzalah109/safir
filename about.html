About Safir ul Quran Academy

Discover our journey, mission, and the dedicated team behind our commitment to Quranic education

Our Mission & Vision

At Safir ul Quran Academy, our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality Quranic education to Muslims worldwide. We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to connect with the Holy Quran, regardless of their location, age, or background.

Founded in 2010, our academy has grown from a small local initiative to an international online platform serving thousands of students across 15+ countries. Our vision is to create a global community of Quran learners who not only recite the words of Allah but understand their meanings and implement them in their daily lives.

We combine traditional teaching methodologies with modern technology to create an engaging learning environment. Our curriculum is designed to cater to students of all levels - from complete beginners to advanced learners seeking specialization in Tajweed or Hifz.
